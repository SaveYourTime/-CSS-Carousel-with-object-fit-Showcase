object-fit

specifies how the contents of a replaced element should be fitted to the box established by its used height and width.

object-fit: fill; take up all the place with it's parent space

object-fit: contain; maintain its aspect ratio fitting within the element’s content box (That's it you can see the whole part)

object-fit: cover; maintain its aspect ratio while filling the element’s entire content box (Either the shorter width or height will take up the place, and the longer one will be crop)

object-fit: none; not resized to fit inside the element’s content box
